Risotto

Description

Risotto is sticky starchy saucy rice dish. Not just any rice. Its arborio. Its risotto.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Parmesan
  • Dry white wine
  • Water, broth, nectar, etc.

Steps

  1. Cook onions in butter on stove
  2. Add Arborio rice and cook for 30 second, no more
  3. Add dry white wine, drink remainder of bottle for health
  4. Slowly add water, broth, nectar, etc. and stir constantly until rice is cooked
  5. Stir in parmesan
  6. Eat it, you won't.
