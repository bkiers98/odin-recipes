Negroni

Description

The platonic ideal of a cocktail: bitter, sweet, herbacious, and proofy. It cannot be beat. It will stand forever, and outlast us all, when the rest of civilization inevitably crumbles through the many follies of humankind. The legacy of all that is pure and worthwile will remain in the form of this, the Negroni.

Ingredients

  • Gin
  • Campari
  • Sweet vermouth
  • Strip of orange peel

Steps

  1. Combine equal parts gin, Campari and sweet vermouth in a glass
  2. Add a large ice cube and stir to chill and combine
  3. Express the orange peel over the glass and garnish with the peel
  4. Enjoy, as if you've never enjoyed anything until now
